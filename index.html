This is Curious Cryptography for Developers

In my initial days as a back-end developer, I saw and heard various words -- salt, cryptographic hash, symmetric keys, public key, private key, TLS and so on. I always had an urge to know what's going around but never did.

Then 5 years back, I got a chance to help freeCodeCamp in creating a short course on public key cryptography aimed at web developers.

Over the time, I have realised that these concepts are kept hidden behind the curtains. But I believe there's many developers that might have the same urge to understand these cryptographic concepts.

So, this course is for every curious developer who ever wondered what's going behind. Welcome!
