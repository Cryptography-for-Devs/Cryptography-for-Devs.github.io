This is Curious Cryptography for Developers

In my initial days as a developer, I saw various words -- salt, cryptographic hash, symmetric keys, public key, private key, TLS and so on. I always had an urge to understand what's going around but never dived in.

Then 5 years back, I got a chance to help freeCodeCamp in creating a short course on public key cryptography aimed at web developers.

I believe there are lots of developers that might have the same urge to understand these cryptographic concepts. So, this mini-course is for all of you, my developer friends!
